Ravioli

Ravioli is the name of a type of Italian stuffed pasta made with different ingredients and usually folded in a square shape. They are accompanied by some type of sauce, especially tomato, tucos, pesto or creams.

Sorrentines

Sorrentinos are a type of Argentine stuffed pasta, similar to ravioli but larger and circular in shape. The dough is made with flour and egg, they usually have a variety of fillings that can combine cheese, ham, vegetables or nuts.
